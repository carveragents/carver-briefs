Head of Compliance
Cybersecurity providers 		Singapore Cyber Trust Mark Requirement

CSA Singapore now requires licensed cybersecurity providers like Qualys to hold the Cyber Trust Mark Promoter Tier. Grace period ends Dec 31 2026. Book certification scope and audit planning immediately. Non-compliance may impact ~2% of Singapore business.

Finance Head
Utility-scale renewable developers 		IRS Renewable Tax Credit Alert

Under IRS Notice 2025-42, wind and solar projects must start real on-site construction by July 5 2026. Lock a physical-work start plan and proof binder immediately. Failure could mean losing up to $5M in tax credits.
