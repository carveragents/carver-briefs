Head of Compliance
Cybersecurity providers 		Singapore Cyber Trust Mark Requirement

The Cyber Security Agency of Singapore now requires licensed cybersecurity providers like Acme Inc. to hold the Cyber Trust Mark "Promoter" (Tier 3)—with a hard grace-period deadline of December 31, 2026. Book your certification scope and audit plan now, working backwards from Dec 31, 2026. Non-compliance can impact roughly 2% of Acme Inc. business in the Country.
Energy CFO
Finance Head
Utility-scale renewables developers 		IRS Renewable Tax Credit Alert

Under IRS Notice 2025-42, utility-scale wind and solar projects must have started real on-site construction—not just spending—by July 5, 2026. Start immediately by locking a "physical-work start" plan and build a proof binder. Miss the standard and Acme Inc. can lose up to $5M in tax credits.
